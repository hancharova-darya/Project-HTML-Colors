About Us

Welcome to Color Psychology, where you can find trusted information about mental health.

Who We Are

Color Psychology is a trusted and compassionate online resource that provides the guidance you need to improve your mental health and find balance. Our library of more than 4,000 pieces of content, created and refined over the past 20+ years, has been written by more than 100 healthcare professionals and industry experts including experienced doctors, therapists, and social workers, and then vetted by board-certified physicians. We give you the resources you need to feel happy and healthy.

Writers And Policies

When it comes to your health and wellness, you need to feel not only confident about the choices you make, but the information that helps you make them. That is why we work with writers who are experts in their areas, from board-certified doctors to credentialed dietitians to licensed therapists and more.

Writers are specifically selected for both their extensive knowledge and real-world experience as well as their ability to communicate complex information in a clear and helpful way. Articles also undergo review from our medical board and routine updating to ensure reporting is responsible, accurate, understandable, helpful, and reflective of the most current research. You can read more on our editorial policies here.

Write For Us

We're always looking for experienced and qualified writers with expertise and credentials in the areas of health and wellness. We have very high standards, something everyone that writes for us is extremely proud of. Sound like you? Email our team.
